Well Laurie didnt come alone from Raviga but also took Monica Hall, Peter's longtime advisor and primary startup hunter, as we can say came with her. She was the one who discovered one buzz of the town Pied Piper.

Currently Ed Chen is the interim head of Raviga. Rumours are it was Chen who arranged the coup by buttering the board. And there are currently a couple of workplace harrassment cases in pipeline against him due to his mistreatment of women.
